Spotlight Member

Michelle Tyler

Backend Developer/
Co-founder

Michelle is one of the Co-founders of LBWT. After moving to Long Beach she noticed a need in the community for women in tech. Women needed a space to network and learn from each other. This year she has organized both social and professional workshops. One of the most popular workshops being the Interview prep series.
